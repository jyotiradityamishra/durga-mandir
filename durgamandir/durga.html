durga puja 2022

Durga Panchmi     30.09.2022

The day idols of Devi Durga along with her
children comes to the Pandal.

Durga sasthi     01.10.2022

Involves rites to awaken and welcome the
goddess to be a guest, known as Bodhon.

Maha saptmi     02.10.2022

Bathing of the navapatrika with holy water
done on the seventh day of the festival.

Maha Ashtmi     03.10.2022

Sandhi Puja, involving the offering of 108
lotuses and lighting of 108 lamps.

Maha Navmi     04.10.2022

The ninth day of festival is marked with
the Homa (Fire oblation) and Bhog.

Vijay Dashmi     05.10.2022

The last day of this festival, called
Bijoya Dashami is marked by Sindoor Khela.
